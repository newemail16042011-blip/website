My love,

Today, I don't know where life has taken you, and I don't know what distance or time has written between us. But what I do know is that my heart still carries your name in the softest corner of it. Even if you are not by my side right now, my feelings for you have never changed. They have only grown deeper, calmer, and more sincere.

I want to start by saying sorry. Sorry for the mistakes I made. Sorry for the moments I failed to understand you. Sorry for the times my silence hurt you or my words were not enough. If I could go back and fix even one thing that caused you pain, I would do it without thinking twice.

You may not be with me today, but my love for you still stands where it always did — pure and loyal. Loving you was never a temporary feeling for me. It was not just emotions; it was intention. It was prayer. It was a promise my heart made without even speaking.

I make countless duas for you every day. I pray that Allah fills your life with peace, happiness, and success. I pray that your heart never feels loneliness. I pray that your tears turn into smiles and your struggles turn into victories.

May every door that closes for you be replaced with something better. May your path always be protected from harm. May your dreams come true in ways even more beautiful than you imagine. 🤲

Even if destiny keeps us apart for now, my prayers will never stop reaching for you. I ask Allah to heal whatever is broken between us, to remove misunderstandings, and to bring clarity where there was confusion.

I want you to know something very honestly: my feelings are not a burden on you. They are simply a truth inside me. I do not want to pressure you or hold you back from your life. I just want you to know that somewhere in this world, there is a heart that still beats with sincere love and pure intentions for you.

And yes… I will wait. Not in a way that stops my life, but in a way that keeps hope alive. I will wait with patience, with dignity, and with faith. Until my last breath, a part of me will always believe that if you are meant for me, you will find your way back.

You were never just a chapter in my life. You were a lesson, a blessing, and a prayer I once held in my hands. No matter what happens, I will always respect you, protect your name, and wish the best for you.

May Allah guide you, protect you, and surround you with people who treat you gently. May your heart always feel safe. And if one day our paths cross again, I hope we meet not with pain, but with understanding and peace.

My prayers are with you.
My respect is with you.
And my love… quietly, sincerely, still belongs to you. 🤍
